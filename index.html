WORK

Our program works by allowing users to take a picture of a disposable item using their phone or other device. Once the picture is taken, the program analyzes the item and returns a result indicating whether it belongs in the recycling, compost, or garbage. To use the program, users simply need to launch the app and take a picture of the item in question. The program will then process the image and provide the user with the appropriate disposal recommendation. This helps users to properly dispose of their waste and reduce the amount of incorrect disposal, which can have negative environmental impacts. The program is easy to use and provides a convenient way for users to ensure that they are disposing of their waste in an environmentally responsible manner.
